Visiting Disney World Orlando - What are the best tickets to purchase?

Find which Disney World Orlando tickets are best for you to purchase for your visit.

by Sara Wilson
Posted on 21st Feb 2023
Post a comment

1. Base tickets: These are the most basic ticket option available and will gain you entry into one of the four major Disney theme parks: Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Hollywood Studios, and Animal Kingdom. This type of ticket is best suited to a day visit but can also be used over multiple days if needed.

2. Park Hopper tickets: Park Hopper tickets allow guests to not only enjoy one park in a single day, but also hop between all four during their stay at Walt Disney World in Orlando. This makes it easy for visitors to enjoy more than one park in a single day and get the most out of their visit.

3. Waterpark & Sports Tickets: Take your vacation to the next level with this great ticket option that offers unlimited access to two amazing water parks and ESPN Wide World of Sports Complex! You’ll be able to slide down Epic Plunge, bob in Castaway Creek or slip down Mach 1 TwisterFlume atDisney’s Blizzard Beach water park and swing away at Hittin’ Wheels batting cages located at the ESPN Wide World of Sports complex when you purchase this incredible pass!

4. Annual Passes: Make an entire year full of magical memories with an annual pass for Walt Disney World Resort in Orlando for yourself or loved ones! This pass gives guests 365-days of admission into any one (or all) of four major theme parks such asMagic Kingdom Park,Epcot, Disney's Hollywood Studios orDisney's Animal Kingdom Theme Park as often as they like throughout the year (with some special restrictions).

5. Memory Maker: Get even more out of your vacation by purchasing a Memory Maker package that allows guests to preserve every magical moment captured during their stay - both on camera and off camera! With the help of Disney PhotoPass photographers stationed throughout the resort area taking complimentary photos with iconic characters and special events such as parades and fireworks shows made even more enjoyable with stunning 3D pictures taken while they are happening!

6. Special Events/Extra Ticketed Experiences: There are always special events going on throughout the year at Walt Disney World Resort in Orlando – from multi-day music festivals like Star Wars Wookiee Weekend Celebration hosted by Galactic Nights to fun Halloween spectacular events termed Mickey Happy Halloween Party – so you might want to explore some extra ticketed experiences entertainment options like these too if you have some extra time during your visit.

7. Discounts/Deals/Offers : Everyone loves saving money - there is no denying that Disney Tickets come packed with hefty price tags involved but there is good news! There are plenty of discounts offered by different retailers out there such as weekly deals at select supermarkets stores online discount portal sites etc., which can make planning your perfect family trip easier on budgets - so don’t forget about those
